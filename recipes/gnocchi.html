Gnocchi

Description

A delicious and easy tomato and gnochi bake featuring savory herbs and cheeses. Using premade gnocchi and canned tomatoes makes this meal a snap

You can customize seasonings however you like to make this dish your own! I personally add extra garlic and a dash of smoked paprika(?) to add a kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 400g chopped tomatoes, with juice
  • 500g gnocchi
  • basil leaves to taste, torn
  • half a ball mozzarella, torn into chunks

Steps

  1. Heat stove to high. Heat olive oil in large, deep frying pan, then soften onion and red pepper for 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in garlic, fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add gnocchi and tomtaoes, bring to a simmer.
  4. Bubble for 10 to 15 minutes, stirring occasionally until the gnocchi is soft and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Season, stir in basil, then transfer to ovenproof dish.
  6. Top with mozzarella, bake for 5 to 6 minutes at 350 degrees F until bubbling and golden.
  7. Remove from oven and allow to cool before serving
